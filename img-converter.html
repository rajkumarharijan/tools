How to Convert Images

  1. Upload your image by dragging & dropping or clicking the upload area.
  2. Select your desired output format (JPG, PNG, WEBP, or GIF).
  3. Click "Convert Image" and wait a few seconds.
  4. Download your converted image in the new format.

Supported Formats

  • JPG (JPEG): Best for photographs and realistic images.
  • PNG: Supports transparency, ideal for logos and graphics.
  • WEBP: Modern format with superior compression.
  • GIF: Used for simple animations and low-color images.
